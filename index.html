WHY AI MATTERS

AI isn’t just for big companies anymore.

AI (Artificial Intelligence) simply means using smart software to handle tasks that used to require a person — like answering questions, booking appointments, following up with leads, or organizing information.

For small businesses in Evergreen, this means you can:

  • Answer customer inquiries even when you're closed
  • Book appointments automatically without back-and-forth emails
  • Follow up with leads consistently (something most businesses struggle with)
  • Free up hours every week that were previously spent on repetitive tasks

The businesses that are starting to use AI now are getting more leads, saving time, and creating a more professional experience for their customers — without needing to hire more staff.
